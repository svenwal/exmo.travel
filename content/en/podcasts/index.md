# 🇩🇪 Camping Caravan Podcast

## Episode 84 – Accidentally Offroad

In this episode of the Camping Caravan Podcast, Sven & Saskia, how an idea turned into a real dream of a lifetime — from selling their house to starting a new, mobile life in an offroad camping truck. They take you along on their first tours through Austria, the Netherlands, France, and Spain, and share their impressions from the road. Alongside personal experiences, they also talk about the technology of their vehicle, the build, and the challenges of everyday life on four wheels. What is it like to live permanently in an offroad truck, what worked well, and what had to be adapted along the way? The first adventures were exciting, educational, and sometimes challenging — but fortunately always with a good outcome. An inspiring episode for everyone who dreams of traveling and living on the road.
